ATM SIMULATOR INTRODUCTION

This program attempts to simulate the simple operation of the ATM machine performing operations like:

  • Send Money (Transfers)
  • Buy Airtime
  • Withdraw Cash
  • Check Balance and
  • Change Pin

Though operations like paying of bills, payarena and quicteller is beyond the scope of this demo.

The program is splitted into 2 levels; The first for the creation of customers and the second for simulating the ATM experience.

There's a need to create customer(s) to test out the machine. The system was built in order to manage the user's and the amount they have with them and how the relationship exists between them. Create a customer or series of customers.

After creating the customer you can then proceed to simulating the ATM machine have fun with the simulation.
